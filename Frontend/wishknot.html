What is a WishKnot?

A WishKnot is a symbolic digital gift: a knotted animated ribbon carrying your custom message, like a hug tied in time. Based on the ancient idea that when you tie a knot, you store your energy and intention within it - to be released when the recipient opens it.

It's pure, visual poetry - perfect for moments when words alone aren't enough.
