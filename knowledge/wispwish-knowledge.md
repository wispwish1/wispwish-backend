## About WispWish

WispWish is an AI-powered digital gift platform that creates personalized emotional gifts like poems, voice messages, illustrations, videos, and songs. We turn your feelings and memories into beautiful digital gifts that can be shared instantly.

**Mission**: Gifts born from the heart, delivered in seconds. You bring the feeling, we turn it into a gift.

**How It Works**: Simply choose an occasion, select your gift type, fill out our customization form with details about your recipient, and our AI creates a personalized gift based on your input. The entire process takes just a few minutes, and your gift is delivered digitally via email or private link.

## Personalization

**How personalized are the gifts?** Every gift is completely unique and personalized based on your input. Our AI considers:
- The recipient's name
- Your relationship
- The occasion
- Tone you want (heartfelt, funny, formal, casual, etc.)
- Specific memories you share
- Any personal messages you include

No two gifts are ever the same!

**Preview Before Sending**: Yes! After our AI creates your gift, you'll see a complete preview. If you're not satisfied, you can request regeneration with specific feedback about what you'd like changed.

**Not satisfied?** No problem! You can regenerate your gift as many times as needed during the preview stage.

## Privacy & Security

**Is my personal information safe?** Absolutely! We use enterprise-grade security, SSL encryption, and never share your personal information with third parties. Your stories, memories, and gifts are completely private and secure.

**What happens to my data?** Your personal information is used only to create your gifts and is stored securely. We never use your data for marketing to others or share it with third parties. You can request data deletion at any time.

**Can others see my gifts?** No! All gifts are completely private. Only you and the people you share the link with can access your gifts. We never display or share your personal gifts publicly.

**Sensitive content handling**: We understand that many gifts contain deeply personal and sensitive information. All content is processed with the highest level of privacy and security. Our AI is designed to handle emotional and sensitive topics with care and respect.
